# EFTA02731023

## Title
EFTA Convention (consolidated text)

## Source
Official source: https://www.efta.int/sites/default/files/documents/legal-texts/efta-convention/efta-convention.pdf

## Summary
This document sets out the foundational rules for cooperation among the member states of the European Free Trade Association. It describes the institutional framework, outlines how decisions are made within the association, and details core principles related to trade in goods and related cooperation. The text serves as the legal baseline for how the member states coordinate and administer the organization.
